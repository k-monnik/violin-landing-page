KRISTEN MONNIK, VIOLINIST

Kristen Monnik is a classically-trained violinist with over a decade of experience playing for weddings and special events. She has performed for hundreds of weddings in the New Orleans area, in several states across the U.S., and is now based in Montréal, Québec. Create an intimate, romantic atmosphere with live music filling the air of your wedding or event. Whether you are planning your wedding at a gorgeous indoor venue, in a vineyard, in Parc Jean-Drapeau, or beyond, a violinist is the perfect way to add an elegant ambiance to your special day.
